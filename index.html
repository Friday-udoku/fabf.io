Why FABF

Desperate times call for desperate measures, a maxim insists. Covid-19 has compelled the government to impose strict restrictions on normal life activities both at the national and state levels in a bid to safeguard lives. This involves the restriction of movement and visitations to public places of which our local libraries are unfortunately inclusive. The restrictions on physical libraries have necessitated a transition to online libraries. Essentially to ensure that students and persons of interest of all classes regarding academic works and books, in general, are not denied access to vital and resourceful materials necessary for their enterprises, academic and non-academic alike, which require the use of the library. Florence Accelerate Book Finder(FABF) is an online alternative to physical libraries designed to satisfy the needs stated above. FABF will allow users to search for books by simply entering a query (title, author, IBN, or description), and it will display the results in a list on the page with a link to the vendor site where the book may be viewed in full detail....

Mission

Florance Accelerated Book Finder is designed to make life easy in difficult times. The sychologycal and mental health impacts of Covid-19 pandemic are disastrous and considering the devastating effects it may have for months and years to come- FABF aims to avail people of the opportunity to manage mental stress and trauma. It will allow visitors to access books and other library materials at their comfort zone for free.

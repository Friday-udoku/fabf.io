About FABF

Desperate times call for desperate measures a maxim insists. The pandemic on rampage in the world at the moment, Covid-19 has compelled the imposition of strict restrictions on normal life activities all over the world by governments both at national and state levels. Florance Accelerated Book Finderis a palliative initiative designed to help people comply with the Covid-Restriction and also to manage the stress and trauma brought by the lockdown.

Mission

With the tremendous sense of uncertainty caused by the worldwide pandemic on individuals and at the global level, the sense of safety and security - including predicability have continued to elude the world and the people in it. Developing or exacerbating a mental health symptoms is inevitable in a situation like this. Florence Accelerated Book Finder(FaBf) is an alternative to physical libraries that aims to avail people with the opportunity to manage mental challenges by allowing visitors to access books of interest at their comfort zone. It provides round the clock access, enabling information to be gathered when it is needed. And the good news is that the service is totally free.

Afiliated | ICANWI

ICANWI stands for Igbo Cultural Association NorthWest Ireland. It is a cultural oriented group founded in 2010 with the shared goal of promoting sustanable peace and unity in the society through cultural integration and giving back to the community through participation and volunteering

ACTIVITIES:

  • Cultural Integration & promotion
  • Charitable Initiative
  • Community Engagement & Volunteering
  • Language Education

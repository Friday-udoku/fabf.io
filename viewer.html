About FABF

Desperate times call for desperate measures a maxim insists. The pandemic on rampage in the world at the moment, Covid-19 has compelled the imposition of strict restrictions on normal life activities all over the world by governments both at national and state levels. Florance Accelerated Book Finderis a palliative initiative that aims to help people in adhering to the Covid-Restriction and managing stress and trauma.
